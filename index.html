Coastal Resort Development

This luxurious coastal resort in Berbera is a testament to Dhismo's ability to create stunning leisure destinations.
The project includes beachfront villas, a world-class hotel, swimming pools, and various recreational facilities,
offering guests an unforgettable vacation experience.
We prioritized environmentally friendly construction methods to preserve the natural beauty of the coastline.

Modern Residential Complex

Dhismo proudly presents a contemporary residential complex in the heart of Hargeisa.
This project features spacious apartments with modern amenities, lush green spaces,
and secure parking, providing residents with a comfortable and convenient urban lifestyle.
We focused on sustainable building practices and energy efficiency in this development.

Commercial Office Tower

This landmark commercial tower in Mogadishu showcases Dhismo's expertise in constructing high-rise structures.
The building offers state-of-the-art office spaces, advanced communication infrastructure, and stunning city views,
making it an ideal location for businesses of all sizes.
The project was completed on time and within budget.

Rural School Renovation

Dhismo is committed to community development. This project involved the complete renovation of a rural school in Burao,
providing students with a safe and modern learning environment.
We focused on creating bright and airy classrooms, updated sanitation facilities, and a new library,
contributing to improved educational opportunities for the local community.
